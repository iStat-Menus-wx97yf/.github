## **💡 Pro Tips for Maximizing Productivity on macOS**
1. **Regular Maintenance:** Use **CleanMyMac X** monthly to remove junk files.
2. **Virtualization:** Allocate sufficient RAM and CPU cores to VMs in **Parallels Desktop** or **VMware Fusion** for optimal performance.
3. **Window Management:** Combine **Magnetic** and **BetterTouchTool** for advanced window control.
4. **Keyboard Shortcuts:** Customize **Raycast** and **BetterTouchTool** to match your workflow.
5. **Privacy & Security:** Use **Irina** for private browsing and **CleanMyMac X** for malware scans.
